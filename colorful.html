About the Award

The Harmony in Diversity Award recognises individuals whose outstanding efforts strengthen social cohesion and harmony between people of different cultures in Southeast Asia.

  • Increase understanding and awareness of social cohesion initiatives.
  • Build a network of Harmony in Diversity champions.
  • Increase cross‑cultural collaborations to amplify community impact.

What the Award Celebrates

We shine a light on individuals whose work demonstrates:

Innovation & creativity

Original approaches to building harmony.

Positive impact & sustainability

Meaningful results that endure.

Courage & resilience

Leading change in challenging contexts.

Long‑term commitment

Consistent, dedicated efforts over time.

Example Areas of Work

  • Interfaith & intercultural dialogue programmes; joint projects; platforms for mutual understanding across social, cultural, religious, or ethnic lines.
  • Arts‑based or creative initiatives; community‑building strategies; technology that connects communities.
  • Storytelling and media that broaden perspectives; cultural exchange; educational initiatives.

Eligibility & Submission Standards

For organisational nominators and ASEAN citizen nominees; English-only submissions.

Who can nominate

Organisations only (e.g., government, intergovernmental organisation, business entity, academic institution, NGO, or other stakeholder).

Who can be nominated

Citizen of any ASEAN Member State (10 countries).

Language & format

English‑only submission via online form, including an impact write‑up and supporting materials (reports, links to visuals, articles, etc.).

Integrity & ethics

Unethical behaviour (misleading information or undue pressure) may result in disqualification and/or revocation.

Selection Criteria — R.A.I.S.E.

Addresses current realities in fostering harmony (e.g., dialogue programmes, joint projects, platforms for understanding).
Aligns with People, Planet, Prosperity, Peace, Partnership; promotes inclusivity, equal opportunity, and a sense of belonging.
Original approaches or concepts (e.g., use of arts, technology, or unique community‑building strategies).
Positive, sustained impact within the local context or beyond.
Improves people’s lives by encouraging broader perspectives and deeper understanding (e.g., storytelling, cultural exchange, educational initiatives).

How to Participate

Simple steps to complete your nomination in English.

STEP 1

Check Eligibility

Your organisation is the nominator; your nominee is a citizen of an ASEAN Member State. Review language and required materials.

STEP 2

Prepare Materials

Draft the impact write‑up and gather supporting documents (reports, links to visuals, articles).

STEP 3

Complete the Online Form

Submit your nomination in English with all materials via the online form.

STEP 4

Verification & Shortlisting

5P Secretariat verifies submissions; shortlisted entries proceed to the Judging Committee.

Award & Recognition

Cash prize, trophy, and regional visibility to elevate your impact.

Monetary & Formal

  • USD 20,000 cash prize and the Harmony in Diversity Trophy.
  • Formal presentation at the award ceremony with Patron and Judges invited.
  • Participation in a curated event to share work and explore potential engagement with a peace‑builder and/or global religious leader.

Visibility & Community (Non‑Monetary Benefits)

  • Media exposure on the award website and official channels; inclusion in announcement and ceremony coverage.
  • Networking with partners, judges, and fellow champions across the region.
  • Learning & knowledge‑sharing through the curated event and post‑award community touchpoints.
  • Platform for impact — opportunities to showcase replicable practices and inspire others.

The Trophy

Sweeping, interlocking curves capture the fluid motion of connection, unity, and mutual uplift. The open, dynamic form reflects an evolving relationship between tradition and modernity — a visual metaphor for harmony in diversity.

Symbolic Elements

  • Interlocking Curves — Collaboration and inclusivity across cultures and ideas.
  • Open Space — Openness, dialogue, and continuous growth.
  • Polished Finish — A mirror of collective achievement; many coming together.
  • Natural Wooden Base — Stability, roots, and a sustainable foundation.
